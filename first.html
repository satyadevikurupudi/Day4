GIET Autonomous

Godavari Institute of Engineering and Technology is the first college of East Godavari district to be granted Autonomous Status. It is permanently affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Kakinada, and was recognized by the University Grants Commission. GIET got the NAAC 'A' grade with 3.31/4 rating from the National Assessment and Accreditation Council.

GIET School Of Pharmacy

GIET School of Pharmacy is a renowned private institute located in Rajahmundry, Andhra Pradesh. The institute offers Degree courses in full-time mode, with a focus on streams such as Medicine and Health Sciences. Students can choose from diverse Doctorate, PG and UG programs which are approved by AICTE, PCI. The institute has a maximum intake of 215 students. It provides education at an affordable fee at an approximate of INR 140,000-240,000. The faculty is trained and experienced and provides students with expert guidance. The institute also has a well-supported campus with modern facilities.
